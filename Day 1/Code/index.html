Basic Black Tea Recipe

✅ Ingredients:
  • cup water
  • teaspoon loose tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
  • teaspoon sugar or honey (optional)
  • ½ cup milk (optional)

Instruction of Black Tea Recipe

✅ Instruction:
  1. Boil Water: Heat 1 cup of water in a pot until it reaches a rolling boil.
  2. Boil Water: Heat 1 cup of water in a pot until it reaches a rolling boil.
  3. Add Milk (Optional): If you like milk tea, pour in ½ cup of milk and let it simmer for another 2 minutes.
  4. Sweeten (Optional): Add sugar or honey as per your taste.
  5. Strain and Serve: If using loose leaves, strain the tea into a cup. If using a tea bag, remove it. Enjoy hot!
